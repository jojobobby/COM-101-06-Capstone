Barriers to Communication

1. Personal Noise

What: Internal distractions like stress or emotion.

Example: You’re upset and can’t focus on what your friend is saying.

2. Physical Noise

What: Environmental noise or distractions.

Example: Can’t hear your teammate on Zoom because of background noise.

3. Semantic Noise

What: Confusion caused by unfamiliar or complex words.

Example: A doctor uses medical terms a patient doesn’t understand.
