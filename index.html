Introduction to Communication

Communication is the process of exchanging information, ideas, or feelings between individuals. It can be verbal, non-verbal, written, or visual.

Two-Way Processing

Communication involves both sending and receiving messages. Feedback is key to ensure understanding and clarity.

Barriers to Communication

  • Personal Noise: Thoughts, emotions, or mental distractions that block understanding.
  • Physical Noise: Loud sounds, environmental factors, or poor signals.
  • Semantic Noise: Confusing language, unfamiliar jargon, or different interpretations.
